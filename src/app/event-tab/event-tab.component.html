Alumni Opportunities

If you have previously attended a Seeing Without Eyes seminar, you are welcome to join future seminars at the same level, at no additional cost for the seminar itself. However, since space is limited, we can only accept alumni for up to 20% of the total class size—so early registration is encouraged.

Alumni will be responsible for covering accommodation, transportation, meals, and seminar materials costs. As part of the experience, returning participants will be paired with new attendees to share insights, answer questions, and help guide them through the process.

Online Practice Sessions for Alumni

After the seminar, alumni can join the live online group practice sessions held twice a week, typically Mondays and Thursdays, to maintain and continue developing their mindsight skills. The first 4 weeks (8 sessions) after the seminar, are free, and after that, participation is available for $100 USD per 4-week (8 sessions) period.
